Why AFLCP?

Traditional centralized machine learning systems require hospitals to share sensitive patient data, leading to privacy, legal, and ethical concerns.

The AFLCP framework solves this by enabling multiple healthcare institutions to collaboratively train a global heart disease prediction model without exposing raw patient data.

Advanced techniques such as FedProx, Top-K communication compression, asynchronous updates, and optional differential privacy ensure scalability, robustness, and high predictive accuracy.
